Story 48 Cuisine Restaurant

Welcome to the 48 Cuisine Restaurant. This restaurant was founded in 2012 with the concept of Asian food fine dining, and cooked by professional chef. Our restaurant provides a variety of Asian dishes including Chinese food, Japanese Food, Korean Food. We have 3 restaurants with 3 different concepts. first outdoor restaurant with a lakeside view. second, a classic concept restaurant with a beautiful rustic feel. third, a restaurant with a modern concept in the middle of the city.
